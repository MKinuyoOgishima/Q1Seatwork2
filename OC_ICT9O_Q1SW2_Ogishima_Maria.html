Crisostomo Ibarra Ibarra was a serious and visionary youthful man. Influenced by his European tuition, he sought to improve the country. As part of that, he believed in the authority of education to effect change and sought to establish a school in San Diego for that purpose.

Maria Clara Maria Clara was a devout Catholic, and is said to have epitomized the virtues of being humble and reserved, devoid of humor and prone to impotence.
Image Name of the Character Description
Capitan Tiago He is from the wealthy Philippines who rubbed his elbows with the power of his time. He symbolizes a wealthy Filipino who oppresses his fellow Filipinos in trade-off for credit and wealth gained through his powerful connections.

Sisa Sisa lives in San Diego and was the mother of Basilio and Crispin. After her two sons went missing, Shytha went mad and she wandered the streets looking for them.
